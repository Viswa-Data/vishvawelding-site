What we do

The team handles structural welding, fabrication, and repair for machinery bases, frames, platforms, gates, and heavy metal components. Whether it is restoring a damaged frame or fabricating a new support structure, each job is executed with the same level of care and precision.

  • Breakdown repair and strengthening for cracked or deformed members.
  • Fabrication of gates, grills, handrails, ladders, and small structures.
  • Alignment correction for machine beds, tracks, and brackets.
  • Shop and on‑site welding support depending on job requirement.

How work is approached

Every job begins with a quick assessment of the problem, rough measurements, and a practical repair or fabrication plan. Joint preparation, welding sequence, and alignment are planned so that the final structure is both strong and visually neat.

  • Site visit or photo‑based assessment to understand the issue.
  • Clear explanation of repair method and approximate timelines.
  • Careful fit‑up, tack welding, and verification before final welds.
  • Grinding, finishing, and touch‑up for a clean final appearance.

Quality & safety focus

Structural safety is taken seriously. Welds are placed with correct penetration and overlap, and stressed areas are reinforced where required. The work area is kept tidy and safe so your staff can continue their tasks around the welding activity.

  • Use of suitable electrodes and parameters for the base material.
  • Attention to distortion control and re‑checking of critical dimensions.
  • Safe handling of power cables, gases, and hot work zones.
